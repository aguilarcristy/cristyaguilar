Girlhood: 13

"13"
Made in Adobe Illustrator

13 is a part of the “Girlhood” series. Inspired by the first time I was catcalled (which happened when I was 13), this piece is a representation of what girlhood is like for women. Catcalling, whistling - these are uncomfortable aspects of life that emerge during our girlhood that leave me with the question - why do * men?

Girlhood is, unfortunately, being catcalled and feeling uncomfortable with your physical presence at the sight of * men.

*some

Created using Adobe Illustrator using Stable Diffusion and Adobe Firefly.
