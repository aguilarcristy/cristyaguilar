ABOUT

Cristy Aguilar is a multi disciplinary designer from the Bay Area.

Focusing on UIUX design, graphic design, and illustration, she works with softwares such as Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Blender to create designs ranging from advertisements to websites, as well as 3D models.


She has previous experience as a gallery intern, social media coordinator, and art instructor. She has also assisted in grant writing for art nonprofits.
