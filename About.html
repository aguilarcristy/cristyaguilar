About

Cristy Aguilar is a multi disciplinary artist and designer from the Bay Area pursuing a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Media Art from San Jose State University.

Focusing on UIUX design, graphic design, and illustration, she works with softwares such as Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, and Blender to create designs ranging from advertisements to websites, as well as 3D models.
Inspired by pink and all things playful, her work reflects current trends in the current world of art and design.

She has previous experience as a gallery intern, social media coordinator, and art instructor.
