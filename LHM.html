Girlhood: La Hija Mayor

"La Hija Mayor"
Made in Adobe Illustrator

La Hija Mayor (The Eldest Daughter) is a part of the “Girlhood Series.” Growing up as the eldest daughter in an immigrant Mexican family changes what your girlhood is like. You grow up hearing what you’re supposed to do for others. You’re supposed to look after your family, help them, and give them your all - even if this puts a halt to your personal goals. Everything you do, you do for them.

Constantly thinking of others, but never yourself leaves you yearning for the day that someone asks you, “What do YOU want to do? What do YOU want to do with your life?”

In many ways, this piece is the letter eldest daughters - las hijas mayores - would like to give their family. My Girlhood has been putting others before me at the cost of living genuinely.

Created using Adobe Illustrator using Stable Diffusion and Adobe Firefly.
